Yes. Bet Guru sends picks and guidance — you place bets yourself on your own FanDuel, DraftKings, Underdog, PrizePicks, BetMGM or Caesars account.

Weekly and Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime from your dashboard; the 5-Day Pass and Lifetime plan are one-time purchases.

No. Past performance never guarantees future results, and all betting carries risk of loss. Only wager what you can afford to lose.
